At the top of the page, you will see options for Login, Sign Up, and Tournaments. If you do not have a profile, please click Sign Up.
On the Sign Up page, you will be asked to enter a username, email address, and password. After successfully signing up, you will be redirected to the Sign In page. Sign in using the credentials you just created (the same username and password you used during sign up).
Once signed in, you will be redirected to the Tournaments page, where a list of tournaments will be displayed.
If you already have an account, simply click Sign In and enter your credentials to be redirected to the Tournaments page.
If you would like to view tournaments without creating an account or signing in, click the Tournaments section on the navigation bar. You will be able to browse the list of available tournaments.
After finding the tournament you’d like to participate in, click on the card image of the tournament. This will redirect you to that specific tournament's page.
On the tournament page, you will find a green Create Team button. Click it and enter the team name as well as the coach’s email. After creating the team, you will see a section in the tournament leaderboard where you can: Input a time for time-based challenges or Enter an integer value for the highest score in the corresponding category.
You can add up to a maximum of 3 players to your team in the player section.
After adding players, click View Team Details to submit your official video entry. Only YouTube or Vimeo links will be accepted.
Once you have submitted your video, input your score for the tournament challenge.
