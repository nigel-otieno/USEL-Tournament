Instructions for Using the USEL Online Tournament

At the top of the page, you will see options for Login, Sign Up, and Tournaments. If you do not have a profile, please click Sign Up.
On the Sign Up page, you will be asked to enter a username, email address, and password. After successfully signing up, you will be redirected to the Sign In page.
Once signed in, you will be redirected to the Tournaments page, where a list of tournaments will be displayed.
If you already have an account, simply click Sign In and enter your credentials to be redirected to the Tournaments page.
If you would like to view tournaments without creating an account or signing in, click the Tournaments section on the navigation bar.
After finding the tournament you’d like to participate in, click on the card image of the tournament to go to that specific tournament's page.
On the tournament page, click the green Create Team button. Enter the team name and coach’s email, and then see your team in the leaderboard.
You can add up to a maximum of 3 players to your team in the player section.
After adding players, click View Team Details to submit your video entry. Only YouTube or Vimeo links are accepted.
Once you have submitted your video, input your score for the tournament challenge.
